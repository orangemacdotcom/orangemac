1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

We may collect information you voluntarily provide when you use the Apps, including:

  • Content you create or submit within the Apps (such as notes in Jot)
  • Communications you send to us, including support requests

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you use the Apps, we may automatically collect certain technical information, including:

  • Device identifiers (such as device type, operating system version, and unique device ID)
  • App usage data (such as features accessed, session duration, and crash reports)
  • Log data (such as IP address, timestamps, and in-app activity)

1.3 Information from Third-Party Services

Some Apps connect to third-party services to deliver their features. We only collect what is necessary to provide those features.

Slice News Reader

Slice connects to RSS feed URLs and podcast providers you add. Feed content is fetched directly from those sources to your device. We do not store your feed list or article content on our servers.

Jot — Plain Text Notes

If you enable iCloud Sync, your notes are stored in your personal iCloud Drive account, managed by Apple. OrangeMac LLC does not have access to notes stored in your iCloud Drive. Apple's Privacy Policy governs how Apple handles your iCloud data.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the Apps and their features

  • To diagnose technical issues and fix bugs

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and may only use it to perform services on our behalf.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or governmental authority, or if we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, the safety of users, or to investigate fraud.
  • Business Transfers: If OrangeMac LLC is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

We do not share your personal data with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.

4. Local and Cloud Storage

Our Apps are designed to keep your data on your device or in your own cloud storage account — not on our servers.

Slice News Reader

Articles and feed data are cached locally on your device. Slice does not upload your reading history or feed subscriptions to OrangeMac servers.

Jot — Plain Text Notes

Notes are stored locally on your device by default. When iCloud Sync is enabled, they are synced through your personal iCloud Drive account. You can disable iCloud Sync at any time in Settings, which reverts to local-only storage.

We retain any collected diagnostic or usage data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law. Content you create within the Apps (notes, saved articles, etc.) remains under your control and can be deleted at any time within the App.
